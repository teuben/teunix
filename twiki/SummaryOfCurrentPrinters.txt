---+ PUBLIC PRINTERS

These printers are for general department use. Ideally you should consult the [[http://localhost:631/printers][localhost:631/printers]] page in your browser on your workstation which printers are available, and where they are located. All printers, except the FAX machine, are on our new 2022 private IP network. 10.255.25.x for ATL and 10.255.24.x for PSC.

Color printers are highlighted in yellow. There are additional private printers on our network, which may appear if you use network discovery to find printers. Please only use the printers listed below. Clicking the printer name link will show the printer status (as long as you are within our *astronet* network). The online list will not show you the IP, you rarely need it. Normally you can use the PRINTER.astro.umd.edu reference. In case of doubt, ping the name.
