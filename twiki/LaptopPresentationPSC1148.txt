---+ Giving a Presentation in PSC 1148 Using Your Laptop
   1 Connect your laptop using the VGA or HDMI cables on the table labelled 'Table HDMI/VGA'.
   1 Touch the CRESTRON touchscreen to wake up the system.
   1 Press the start button.
   1 On the main menu, find 'Table HDMI/VGA' on the upper horizontal bar and press it.
   1 Then touch 'Monitor' on the lower horizontal bar. Your laptop screen should now display on the monitor.
   1 You can select the 'Lights' tab on the right to change the lighting in the room. Select any of the Presets or turn off all the lights.
   1 When finished, select 'Shutdown' on the CRESTRON touchscreen.
