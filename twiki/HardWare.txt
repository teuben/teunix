---++ Hardware Information

This is the one-stop shop for all information that pertains to hardware, which includes public workstations, printers, scanners, projectors, laptops, computing clusters, Apple TV, etc. If you need to find a printer, this is the place to look! The question which is the best hardware to use for your problem, is often left open, but we now have some guidelines to [[Pick Your Computing Platform]]

   * [[Public Workstations]] : A list of machines anybody in the department can use. (the command *host-info public* should be used for this) 
      * [[Student Workstations]] : A policy document regarding public workstations located on a student's desk.
   * [[Printing Info]] : Everything to do with printers.
   * [[Department Laptops]] : Info on the laptop computers that the Astronomy Department has available.
   * [[Cluster Computing]] : Description of the cluster computing resources available, including the [[Yorp Cluster]] (yorp/astra) and [[IT Computing Cluster]] (zaratan)
   * [[VisLab]] : Information on the visualization lab.
   * [[UnmanagedComputers]] : if you bring in your own computer hardware
   * [[AppleTVinLibrary][AppleTV in Library]] : How to connect wirelessly to the TV in the library.( *deprecated*)
