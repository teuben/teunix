---+ Weekly events at the Department of Astronomy

This is an overview of the mostly weekly events. If there is an event missing, or if the information is out of date, please edit accordingly.
| *Day* | *Time* | *Location* | *Name* | *Description* |
| Monday | 11:00 | PSC 1136 | <a target="_blank" href="https://www.astro.umd.edu/events/colloquia/theory.html" title="CTC Lunch">CTC Lunch</a> | Talks by postdocs of other universities, sometimes people from our university |
| Monday | 11:00 | ATL 1250 | <a target="_blank" href="https://www.astro.umd.edu/events/colloquia/planetary.html" title="PALS">PALS</a> | Planetary Astronomy Lunch Series, discussing small-bodies, planets, exoplanets, satellites, rings, and circumstellar disks |
| Monday | 16:00 | PSC 1148 | <a target="_blank" href="https://www.astro.umd.edu/twiki/bin/view/GradLife/JournalClub" title="Journal Club">Journal Club</a> | Discussion of a paper by the colloquium speaker by the grad students |

| Tuesday | 16:00 | PSC Lobby | <a target="_blank" href="https://umdphysics.umd.edu/events/physicscolloquia.html" title="Physics Colloquium">Physics Colloquium</a> | The weekly physics colloquium, sometimes on astronomical topics |
