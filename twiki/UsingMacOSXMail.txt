Instructions for Apple Mail. The following steps worked on OSX 10.11 and 10.12:

- Go to System Preferences;

- Select Internet Accounts;

- Selected Add Account (the plus sign);

- Scroll down the list and click 'Add Other Account';

- Select Mail Account;

- Fill in your username, password and email (ie, &lt;username&gt;@astro.umd.edu, but without &lt;&gt;);

- It will complain it needs more details:

*Account type: IMAP*

*Incoming Mail: mailhost.astro.umd.edu*

*Outgoing mail: mailhost.astro.umd.edu*

That should be it.
